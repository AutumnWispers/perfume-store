How to Choose the Perfect Perfume

Choosing the right perfume is important because fragrance becomes part of your personality. A good perfume should match your lifestyle, mood, and personal style.

Understand Fragrance Notes

Perfumes are made of three layers: top notes, middle notes, and base notes. These combine to create a long-lasting scent.

Choose According to Occasion

Light floral or citrus perfumes are perfect for daily wear, while woody or oud fragrances work better for evening events.

Test Before Buying

Always test perfume on your skin because fragrance reacts differently with body chemistry.
